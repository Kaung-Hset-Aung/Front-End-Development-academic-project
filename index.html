Pollution and its Impacts

Pollution has become a global problem as all living organisms, from unicellular microbes to the largest blue whales, including men, have to depend on Earth's supply of air, water, soil and minerals for their survival.

When these resources had come to pollution, all form of life becomes threatened and many environmental organizations are starting to focus on building a cleaner and greener environment together with the government. Apart from government's effort and organizational programs, individual cooperation of every citizen as a part is also of high significance.

>

Adopting sustainable practices and embracing a mindset that values environmental stewardship is no longer a choice; it is a responsibility we owe to ourselves and to our future generations.Simply, pollutions are signs and symptoms of mother earth's sickness and are urgently demanding humanity to give effective attention on building a clean and green community.

There is a countless number of buildings in a city and none of them is built up with one single brick. The taller the building measures, the more the amount of bricks constitute, such that the larger clean and green community we desire to build, the bigger network of cooperative participants we demand.

Role of initiative youths

The role of initiative youths here is to promote the interest of people on our green projects and convince them for their cooperation. In order to accomplish our aim, we have to educate our community about the nature of pollutions that our environment has been suffering from, and let them aware that the chief causes of pollution were brought about by the population itself as a result of excessive and erroneous use of natural resources.

We should suggest a general idea about some dos and don'ts towards our community, that one may finally realize how sustainable use of natural resources will ameliorate pollutions, how each individual could cooperate, and why their cooperation is important in creating a cleaner and greener community. As a result, the amount of unnecessary waste of resources will be greatly reduced and the process of sustaining will come as a successful outcome.
