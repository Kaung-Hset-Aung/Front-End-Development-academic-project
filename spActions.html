What has Singapore Polytechnic done?

4 Green Mark Platinum Buildings

T18A, T18B, T3B and the SP Sports Arena have been certified Green Mark Platinum with on-going efforts to certify all 22 buildings in SP by 2025.

230 Species of Flora


SP's lush campus is home to an incredible biodiversity of plants including our majestic Brown Woolly Fig Heritage Trees found near Food Court 1.

110+ Water Dispensers


SP cultivate eco-conscious habits by encouraging students and staff to use water dispensers around campus and have ceased the use of plastic bottled water at our events.

500+ Smart Sensors

Many facilities on campus are monitored with smart sensors to track human traffic and monitor air quality for the health and well-being of students and staff.

4 Smart Offices

Enhancing the work environment for the health and well-being of SP's staff through air quality and temperature sensors in our offices.

13 Diplomas featuring Common Core Curriculum (CCC)

The CCC prepares SP's students for the green economy by exposing them to inter-disciplinary learning experiences and global issues, framed by the SG Green Plan and the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals, where students will learn to develop prototype sustainability solutions for real-life issues.

SP Environment Club

SP offers over 100 clubs for CCA and one of them is SP Environment Club, which always initiate green and clean activities on campus and beyond.
